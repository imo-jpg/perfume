Perfume Thoughts

updated ad-hoc

Inexcusable Evil

by Toskavat

stay away

Smells like what I imagine cleaning a gunshot wound might smell like. Terrifying in the bottle; strangely okay on the skin *only* after a few hours.

Notes: fear; iodine; blood; metal

Did I like it? No.

sample

The Point

by Clue

a snack by the ocean

Unctuous and a bit off-putting on me, like eating an oyster and letting it sit in your mouth a beat too long. A hint of jasmine if you really go looking for it.

Notes: cucumber; oysters; salt; ocean; jasmine

Did I like it? Sort of.

sample

Cherry Punk

by Room 1015

reminds me of a close friend

A femme biker taking off her helmet and shaking out her hair. Either her jacket or her hair is red. On me, fairly sweet at first, then overpoweringly leathery.

Notes: cherry; leather; pepper

Did I like it? It was ok.

Kyoto

by Commes de Garçons

alluring

Smells like the coolest person you know. Or an intriguing stranger on the subway/tube/metro/u-bahn. Creamy on my skin at first, then sharp.

Notes: wood; pepper; iso e super

Did I like it? Yes!

Bibliotheque

by Byredo

warm and sweet

Sweet and fizzy like a blackberry Ahoj Brause. A little vanilla-y after that. Something almost like candlewax and a little leather underneath the sweetness.

Notes: blackberry candy; wax; leather; vanilla

Did I like it? Yes.

You Or Someone Like You

by État libre d'Orange

clean and fresh

Minty! Zesty! Smells like a coworker who never sweats, even in a full suit, even in the summer.

Notes: mint; jasmine; office A/C; citrus

Did I like it? Yes.

Blanche

by Byredo

almost overpowering

I'm getting something dry and powdery and so, so clean. Like someone whose skin probably feels like it's covered in a layer of talcum powder. Very floral and soapy, but bar soap after it's been sitting out for a bit to dry, not liquid soap.

Notes: soap; white flowers; dust

Did I like it? It was ok.

Gypsy Water

by Byredo

creamy and sweet

Smells either like the kids' stationary section of a high-end department store, or a well-stocked rug shop. Then there's something slightly sharp and acidic or peppery underneath it all.

Notes: sandalwood; pepper; vanilla; orange peel

Did I like it? Yes.

Rose of No Man's Land

--

Rose first, then a stall in an upscale shopping mall, then pepper and BO, then...pickles?

Gentle Fluidity (Silver Edition)

by Maison Francis Kurkdjian

too strong

Soapy, clean, but not at all floral. Smells a bit like the samples you get by unsticking two pages in a magazine. A little chemically, a little peppery like cilantro.

Notes: soap; cilantro

Did I like it? It was ok.

sample

Mojave Ghost

by Byredo

mature

Fruity, juicy, a little lipstick or playdough or a school classroom’s plastic chair/wooden desk combo underneath. Smells like an older, single mother of a toddler who lives in a well-appointed Altbau apartment in Berlin and has a high-paying job in a creative or academic field.

Notes: berries; wax

Did I like it? It was ok.

sample

Bal D'Afrique

by Byredo

delicious

Creamy, woody, grassy; like really high-quality, cream-colored paper that you could imagine writing a letter on with a fountain pen and sealing into an envelope with a blood-red wax stamp.

Notes: wood (cedar? sandalwood?); citrus peel

Did I like it? Yes!

sample

Rabbit Rabbit

by Pearfat Perfumes

sticky

First: sharp and peppery, or like raisins soaked in rum; then: like walking down the bath bomb aisle at LUSH; last: it lingers like you spilled POG juice on yourself.

Notes: pink peppercorns and cardamom; tropical fruits

Did I like it? Yes.
